Treeple!

You are presented with a grid of 9 images of barks, buds, and leaves of 3 trees (shown as clues). Your task is to identify each of 3 groups (bark, bud and leaf) in utmost 4 wrong attempts.

Select 3 images - one of bark, bud and leaf - from the grid that you think belongs to the same tree and click Submit to check if your guess is correct. Find all the 3 groups without making 4 mistakes. You can get hints to solve it.

Click the New Game button to begin
